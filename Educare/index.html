Transforming Healthcare Training with Immersive AR/VR Experiences

Medical Tool Familiarization

This immersive AR experience introduces users to essential medical instruments such as stethoscopes, tweezers and injections. The simulation provides a detailed visualization of each tool, highlighting its individual parts and functions. Users can interact with the equipment in a hands-on manner, gaining an intuitive understanding of its components and operation. Designed for educational and training purposes, this simulation aims to enhance the familiarity of students with critical medical instruments.

Injection Simulation

The Injection Simulation AR provides a comprehensive and interactive guide on administering injections accurately. This simulation demonstrates the mechanics of how injections work and offers step-by-step visual guidance on identifying the appropriate sites for injecting into veins or arteries. It aims to help medical students, trainees, and professionals practice and refine their technique in a risk-free, immersive environment.

CBC Simulation

The CBC Simulation AR offers an immersive exploration of blood composition, focusing on key components like Red Blood Cells (RBCs), White Blood Cells (WBCs), and platelets. Users can navigate through a simulated blood vessel, interact with each blood cell type, and receive detailed information on their structure, function, and significance. This interactive simulation provides a visually engaging and educational experience, designed to help students deepen their understanding of blood components and their roles in the circulatory system.
